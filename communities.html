Communities for Kerala & Indian students in Australia

Koott connects Malayali and Indian students before they even land in Australia. Whether you're planning your move from Kochi, Thrissur, Ernakulam, Kozhikode, Kannur or Thiruvananthapuram, or already settling into Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Perth or Newcastle, there's a free community for your city, university, intake and course.

Ways to find your people

  • By interest — course and field circles (IT, healthcare, engineering, business and more) so you meet students exploring the same path.
  • By city — Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Perth and Newcastle student rooms for housing leads, part-time jobs and meetups.
  • By university — connect with your cohort at UNSW, Monash, the University of Melbourne, UTS, Deakin and RMIT.
  • By intake — February and July intake groups, so you arrive already knowing people on the same journey.

From Kerala to Australia, together

Moving abroad from Kerala is a huge step. Inside each community, students a step ahead share what actually matters — finding accommodation, opening a bank account, your 48-hour fortnightly work limit, OSHC health cover, and the 485 post-study work visa. It's the കൂട്ട് (koott — "together") you wish you'd had on day one.

New here? Find your course with the free AI advisor, browse verified WhatsApp groups for Kerala & Indian students, or read the funding & insurance guide and the first-week arrival kit.
