🔢 Get your Tax File Number (TFN)
You need a TFN to work legally and to be taxed correctly. Apply after you arrive, online via the ATO — it's 100% free and posted to your Australian address in ~28 days. Never pay an agent for this.

You'll need your passport and an Australian postal address (your accommodation is fine). Apply directly here:

Apply for a TFN — ATO ↗ ato.gov.au ↗

Give your TFN to your employer and bank once it arrives — without it you'll be taxed at the highest rate.
